Project Overview

Garpus AI is a comprehensive business intelligence platform designed specifically for small businesses. It leverages advanced machine learning algorithms to provide accurate sales forecasts, market trend analysis, and actionable marketing insights that help businesses make data-driven decisions and optimize their operations.

Key Features

  • • AI-powered sales forecasting
  • • Market trend analysis
  • • Customer behavior insights
  • • Marketing campaign optimization
  • • Inventory management recommendations
  • • Real-time business analytics dashboard

Technologies

  • • Python & scikit-learn/TensorFlow
  • • Time series forecasting models
  • • Natural Language Processing
  • • React.js for frontend
  • • PostgreSQL database
  • • AWS cloud infrastructure

System Architecture

Data Layer

AI Engine

ML models for forecasting and predictive analytics

Insights Layer

Actionable recommendations and visualizations

Core Features

Sales Forecasting

Advanced time series models predict future sales trends with high accuracy, helping businesses plan inventory and staffing.

Market Analysis

Comprehensive market trend analysis and competitive intelligence to identify growth opportunities and market shifts.

Customer Insights

Marketing Optimization
